    Department Of Arts-Course:Sociology

Sociology includes the study of these interlinked components of the society and analyses them concerning various personal, political, environmental, and economic matters.The purpose of studying sociology is to devise methods, theories, and connections between numerous variables working related to each other and understanding their working.

It is a full-time three-year undergraduate degree course offered by the Department Of Arts in Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology.

Eligibility:

The candidate must have passed in 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized Central / State Board with 50% marks in aggregate and should have born on or after 01.07.1998.
